Monsters

All monsters overview

Rift Herald is an epic monster on the northern side of the map that requires multiple champions to defeat. He spawns at 4 minutes and is replaced by Baron Nashor at around 20 minutes.

Baron Nashor is an epic monster on the northern side of the map that requires multiple champions to defeat. He spawns at 20 minutes and replaces the Rift Herald.

He uses many abilities to wear your team down, including corrosive acid that temporarily ignores resistances, and high damage abilities that can surprise even the best-equipped team.
